Common Questions

Q: Does the extension store my documents?
A: No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Nothing is stored or transmitted externally.

Q: Why does the extension ask for Google Docs access?
A: It needs to read your document structure and apply your chosen formatting. Access is limited to this function only.

Q: How do I uninstall the extension?
A: Go to chrome://extensions, find “Docs Style Explorer,” and click Remove.
